HTML CSS Interview Questions

HTML Interview Questions

  1. What are the key differences between block, inline, and inline-block elements?
  1. Explain semantic HTML. Why is it important?
  1. When would you use <section>, <article>, <aside>, and <main> respectively?
  1. What is the difference between <div> and <section>?
  1. How do <header>, <nav>, and <footer> improve accessibility and SEO?
  1. What are the differences between <ol>, <ul>, and <dl>?
  1. What’s the difference between id and class attributes? When do you use each?
  1. Explain the alt attribute on images.
  1. Contrast <em>, <strong>, <i>, and <b>.
  1. What are the roles of for, id, name, value, and required in forms?
  1. Explain the difference between placeholder and <label>. Why keep both?

CSS Interview Questions

Core & Foundations

  1. Explain the CSS box model and how box-sizing affects it.
  1. What are the differences between content-box and border-box?
  1. Compare relative, absolute, fixed, and sticky positioning (behavior & stacking).
  1. Explain the difference between display: none and visibility: hidden.
  1. How does z-index work and why does it sometimes “not work”?
  1. Compare px, em, rem, vw, vh, %—and when to use each.

Selectors & Specificity

  1. Explain CSS specificity. Order of precedence among type, class, id, inline styles.
  1. How do pseudo-classes (e.g., :hover, :focus, :nth-child) differ from pseudo-elements (e.g., ::before, ::after)?
  1. How do descendant, child (>), adjacent sibling (+), and general sibling (~) selectors differ?
  1. Strategies to avoid over-specificity and reduce the need for !important.

Layout: Flexbox & Grid (basic only)

  1. When would you pick Flexbox over Grid and vice versa?
  1. In Flexbox, explain flex-direction, justify-content, and align-items.
  1. How do flex-grow, flex-shrink, and flex-basis interact? Provide common pitfalls.
  1. In Grid, how do grid-template-columns/rows and repeat() work?
  1. Explain grid item placement via grid-column and grid-row.
  1. How do you center content both horizontally and vertically using Flexbox?
