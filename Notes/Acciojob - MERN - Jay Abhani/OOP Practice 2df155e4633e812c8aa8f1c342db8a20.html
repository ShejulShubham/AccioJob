Rubric (For All Assignments)

  • Correct Use of Pillars: Uses only the required concepts for that task.
  • Encapsulation: Private fields/methods are not accessed directly.
  • Inheritance: Proper use of extends; super in subclass constructors where needed.
  • Polymorphism: One common method name; different behaviors in subclasses; iterated through a common interface.
  • Abstraction: Complex steps hidden behind a simple public method.
  • Validation & Messages: Reasonable logs for invalid inputs or blocked operations.
  • Clean Demo: Minimal console demo showing correct behavior (no solutions filled in core logic beyond TODOs).
